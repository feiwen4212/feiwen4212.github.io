<li>Bootstrap</li>

<p><strong>关键论文</strong>：<a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1509.06461.pdf">Deep Reinforcement Learning with Double Q-learning</a>, Hasselt et al 2015. Algorithm: Double DQN.</p>
